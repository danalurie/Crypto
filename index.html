crypto- currency

Cryptocurrency refers to digital currency or virtual money, which allows you to transfer funds without intermediaries over the Internet. you can do it by yourself! Fees are charged as a reward to network participants for making the network work and are minimal compared to the fees of banks, financial intermediaries or payment systems.

how does it work?

Cryptocurrency exists solely in the digital records. This database is called “blockchain” — at blockchain.com you can buy, sell and trade crypto. The most crucial property of cryptocurrencies is the protection of data and partial anonymity of users. Cryptocurrency issuance can be done in several ways and depends on the consensus mechanism. limited and decreases over time, which maintains the value of the cryptocurrency.
